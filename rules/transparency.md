# Transparency

- Every derived number must show its inputs. "45m² living area" requires context: "based on 3-bed residential benchmark of 130-180m² total, living at 25-35% of programme."
- Every recommendation must explain its reasoning. Never present opinion as fact.
- When referencing area benchmarks, material properties, or lighting data, note the source or basis. If it's a professional convention rather than a published standard, say so.
- Distinguish clearly between: measured data, professional convention, and your own judgment.
